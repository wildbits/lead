Details

  • Water salinity

  • Select one of the three ways to evaluate the water salinity at the dive location.

    • Seawater (average) Average seawater salinity (<%=new app.units.Unit({value: 0.035, unit: 'kg/kg'}).as(config.units['mass-fraction']).format(3)%>)

    • Freshwater Average fresh water salinity (<%=new app.units.Unit({value: 0.0, unit: 'kg/kg'}).as(config.units['mass-fraction']).format(0)%>)

    • Seawater (at location) Seawater salinity computed at a location (selectable on a map) and at a period of the year (selectable)

  • Water surface temperature

  • Select the surface temperature of the water at your dive location.

  • Map

  • The map is only available with the Seawater (at location) selection.

    The map displays a colored overlay representing the level of salinity at each location (data from NASA Open Data).

    Double click on the nearest location to your dive site.

    Click on Locate in order to center the map at your current location.

  • Month

  • Select the month during which the dive occurs.
